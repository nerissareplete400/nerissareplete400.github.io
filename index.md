## 💡 Tips for better results

The quality of your conversion depends on the quality of your images. Follow these tips to improve your success:

* Use clear scans: Blurry images make it hard for the software to recognize letters. 
* High resolution: Scan documents at 300 dots per inch for the best accuracy.
* Good lighting: If you take a photo of a page, ensure the light hits the paper evenly. Avoid shadows across the text.
* Check language settings: The software reads different languages. Make sure to select the language of your document before you start the conversion.
